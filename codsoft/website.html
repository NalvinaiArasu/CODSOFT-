LOCATION BASED ALARM SYSTEM

A location-based alarm is a feature in some apps that allows users to set reminders or alarms based on their physical location. Instead of setting a specific time for the alarm to go off, users specify a geographical area, and when they enter or leave that area, the alarm triggers. It's handy for tasks like remembering to pick up groceries when passing by a store or reminding oneself of a task upon arriving or departing from a particular location.
